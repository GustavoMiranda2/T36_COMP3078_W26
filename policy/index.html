Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 1, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how BrazWebDes Hairstylist Booking collects, uses, stores, and protects information when you use our mobile app, website, and related booking services.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following categories of information:

  • Account information such as your name, email address, and password.
  • Booking information such as selected services, appointment dates, times, notes, and booking history.
  • Profile and testimonial information that you choose to submit through the app.
  • Technical information needed to operate the service, such as basic device, browser, and log data.

2. How We Use Information

We use collected information to:

  • Create and manage user accounts.
  • Schedule, confirm, update, and cancel appointments.
  • Provide customer support and service-related communications.
  • Improve app reliability, functionality, and user experience.
  • Protect the service against misuse, fraud, or unauthorized access.

3. Communications

We may send operational messages related to your bookings, such as confirmations, reminders, rescheduling notices, and cancellation updates.

4. Sharing of Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information only when reasonably necessary to operate the service, including with hosting, infrastructure, and email delivery providers, or when required by law.

5. Data Storage and Security

We use reasonable administrative and technical measures to protect personal information. No internet-based system can be guaranteed to be completely secure, but we work to reduce risk and limit unauthorized access.

6. Data Retention

We keep personal information for as long as needed to provide the booking service, maintain business records, resolve disputes, enforce our policies, and meet legal obligations.

7. Your Choices

You may be able to:

  • Update account details through the app or by contacting us.
  • Request deletion or correction of your personal information, subject to legal or operational requirements.
  • Stop using the app at any time.

8. Children’s Privacy

This service is not intended for children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will post the revised version on this page and update the effective date.

10. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or your information, contact: admin@brazwebdes.com
